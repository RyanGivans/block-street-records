Built for people who still want to feel music.

Block Street Records is a local music landmark with the warmth, grit, color, and personality that cannot be faked. It is a shop built around discovery, conversation, memory, and the simple joy of flipping through records by hand.

Walk in for one album. Leave with a stack, a story, and a reason to come back.

Not a feed. Not an algorithm. A real place.
